Background

I’m engineer, expert of printing and publishing. After the post-pc era, the way of doing communication has dramatically changed, so I understood that I had to reinvent my skills, since the interest of people was quickly moving from printed paper to something much more attractive. For this reason, I started to study again, combining my previous experience of publisher together with my first vocation, engineering. And I realized that making websites is much more complex, as well engaging, than making a magazine or a book.

So, I started to frequent online courses trying to go as deeper as possible. I mastered several tools from Adobe CC, and I did my experience in creating websites using the Business Catalyst platform.

I like to have full control of any process, and the more I study, the more I become hungry, because I am really taken from this universe. So I decided to accept Treehouse challenge, and I subscribed to this Teachdegree. And I like to believe that if things will go in the right way, I will try to master more and more arguments through Teachdegree experiences.
